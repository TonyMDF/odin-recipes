OREO Cookie Balls

Description

Can't decide between cookies and confections? You don't have to!
These chocolate-covered cookie balls feature a filing of cream cheese and
crushed cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 36 OREO Cookies, finely crushed
  • 16 ounces semi-sweet baking chocolate, melted

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese and cookie crumbs unti well blended.
  2. Shape into 48 (1-inch) balls. Freeze 10 min.
  3. Refrigerate 1 hour or unti firm.
